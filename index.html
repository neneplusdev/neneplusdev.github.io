About Nayana

After embarking on her journey post-graduation, she delved into the realm of software development, honing her skills for a remarkable 5 years within the confines of a dynamic software company. As a dedicated full stack developer, she immersed herself in a myriad of technologies, from Angular to Node JS, mastering platforms like Wordpress and Magento with finesse. Her expertise extended beyond coding; she adeptly managed servers, facilitated project deployments, led teams, and fostered seamless client communication.
After half a decade of enriching employment, she ventured into the realm of freelance and contractual work, embracing remote collaboration for over 3 years. Notably, she contributed her talents to esteemed entities such as Dell Technologies, where she seamlessly integrated into agile methodologies as a contractor Angular developer.
Beyond her professional prowess, she possesses an array of personal strengths. Discipline, organization , and a penchant for cultivating a harmonious atmosphere characterize her demeanor. A calm and compassionate listener, she excels in effective communication, embodying an introverted yet warm disposition.
Outside the realm of technology, she finds solace in the pages of self-help books and the nurturing embrace of her indoor garden. Methodically scheduling her weeks with precision, she orchestrates her life with intention and purpose.
During her leisure moments, she indulges in the simple pleasures of nurturing her plants, delving into enriching literature, and immersing herself in nature documentaries. Engaging with loved ones and embarking on spontaneous trips to natural havens with her husband and family rejuvenates her spirit, adding vibrant hues to the canvas of her life.
